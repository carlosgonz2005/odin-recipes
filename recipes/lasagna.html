Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a wide,flat sheet of pasta. It has several layers of pasta with sauce and other ingredients. The most common indgredients that are included in lasagna is meat and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna
  • Egg
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Onion
  • Parsley

    • Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine Cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
